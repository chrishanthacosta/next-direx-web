About Us

Who We Are

NextDirex Solutions, based in Calgary, Alberta, delivers innovative and client-focused services across Canada and around the world. We specialize in GIS services and custom software development.

Industries We Serve

Our Mission

Our mission is to help companies overcome their business challenges by providing simple, actionable solutions that enable them to move forward with confidence. We focus on building long-term partnerships and delivering practical solutions that create real business value.

Our Vision

Empowering businesses to grow sustainably through trusted partnerships, innovative solutions, and reliable services.

Our Team

Our team consists of highly skilled solutions experts who deliver services efficiently, effectively, and customized to meet your business needs. Founded in 2025, NextDirex Solutions brings decades of combined experience from our team's previous roles, providing expert guidance and reliable results from day one.

The team includes four leadership roles overseeing GIS services, software development and geospatial development. Each leader brings over 20 years of hands-on experience across our core service areas.

Our Impact

Over the years, we have supported numerous clients across Canada in the mining industry by providing GIS services and developing custom software and mapping applications.
