About Us

Who We Are

NextDirex Solutions, based in **Calgary, Alberta**, delivers innovative and client-focused services across Canada and around the world. We specialize in GIS services and custom software development, and also provide reliable trade support. Founded in 2025, our team brings **decades of combined experience**.

Industries We Serve

We primarily serve the **mining industry** , and also support **tourism, agriculture** and other sectors. Our trade services focus specifically on **agricultural crops and fertilizers**.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission:

To help companies overcome their business challenges by providing simple, actionable solutions that enable them to move forward with confidence.

Vision:

Empowering businesses to grow sustainably through trusted partnerships, innovative solutions, and reliable services.

Our Expert Team

Our team consists of **highly skilled solutions experts**. The team includes **four leadership roles** overseeing GIS services, software development, geospatial development and trader relations, with each leader bringing **over 20 years of hands-on experience**.
